Business Description
Aggie Veterinary Consult is a specialized veterinary pharmacy and farm supply store focused primarily on poultry health and management. We provide a comprehensive range of animal health products, including essential medicines, vaccines, and supplements, alongside farm tools and high-quality feeds. We also specialize in the supply of Day Old Chicks (DOC) to poultry farmers.

Our mission is to support local farmers by ensuring access to quality, affordable veterinary products and expert advice to ensure the health and productivity of their livestock.

2. Project Goals
We are looking to develop a professional website to establish an online presence, showcase our product catalog, and serve as a resource for our customers.

Key Objectives:

Brand Visibility: Establish Aggie Veterinary Consult as a trusted leader in the local poultry and veterinary sector.
Product Catalog: Display our range of products (Drugs, Vaccines, Feeds, Tools, Supplements) so farmers can see what we stock.
Customer Education: Share basic guides on poultry health, vaccination schedules, and farm management.
Location & Contact: Make it easy for customers to find our physical shop and contact us for orders.
3. Target Audience
Poultry Farmers: Small to large-scale poultry farmers in the Oduom and greater Kumasi area.
Pet Owners: Local residents looking for basic veterinary supplies.
Livestock Keepers: Farmers raising other livestock (goats, sheep, cattle) requiring health supplements and feeds.
4. Key Website Features Needed
A. Home Page
Hero Section: High-quality images of healthy poultry/farm setting with a clear value proposition.
Featured Categories: Quick links to Drugs, Vaccines, Feeds, and DOC.
About Snippet: Brief introduction to the business and its location.
Testimonials: Reviews from satisfied farmers (if available).
B. Product Showcase (Catalog)
Categories:
Drugs: Antibiotics, dewormers, vitamins.
Vaccines: Viral and bacterial vaccines (with storage handling info).
Feeds: Starter, grower, finisher formulations.
DOC (Day Old Chicks): Information on breeds available and booking schedules.
Tools: Drinkers, feeders, crates, etc.
Supplements: Growth boosters, egg production enhancers.
C. Services & Resources
Consultation Info: How to book a vet visit or consultation.
Farming Tips: Blog or resources section (e.g., "Vaccination Schedule for Broilers").
D. Contact & Location
Interactive Map: Google Maps integration pointing to Asonaba Goil Station.
Contact Form: For general inquiries.
Click-to-Call: Prominent phone number button for mobile users.
WhatsApp Integration: Direct chat link for quick ordering.
5. Design Preferences
Color Palette: Green (growth/agriculture), White (cleanliness/health), and perhaps accents of Orange/Yellow (poultry).
Style: Clean, professional, and mobile-responsive (crucial, as most farmers use mobile phones).
Imagery: Authentic photos of the shop, products, and healthy livestock rather than generic stock photos.
6. Technical Requirements
CMS: WordPress or a lightweight custom solution for easy content updates.
Mobile Optimization: Must load fast and look perfect on smartphones.
SEO: Optimized for local search terms like "Poultry drugs in Oduom", "Vet shop Kumasi", "Day old chicks supplier".
7. Future Scalability
Potential to add proper E-commerce functionality (Online ordering & Delivery) in Phase 2.
